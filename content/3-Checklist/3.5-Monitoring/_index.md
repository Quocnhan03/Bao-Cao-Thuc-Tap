- **3.5.1. Monitoring:**  
  Configure CloudWatch Log Groups for services such as Lambda and Glue to collect and store system logs.  
  Set up CloudWatch Dashboards to monitor system performance, errors, and overall operational status.

- **3.5.2. Alerting:**  
  Configure Amazon SNS to send notifications via Email or SMS when incidents occur or when defined thresholds are exceeded (e.g., Lambda errors, ETL job failures, or budget limits).
